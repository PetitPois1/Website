📖 How to Play

🍺 Tavern

Use Gems to pull characters. The pool gets better as you beat Campaign stages.

⚔️ Voyage

Beat stages to earn Gems/Wood. If stuck, level up or re-arrange your team. Slot 1 is Captain, and their Type (👊/🔫/⚔️) affects the whole team's damage!

⚓ Hub (Shipyard/Forge)

Upgrade your ship for global buffs. Upgrade Gear at the Forge using Wood.

⛓️ Impel Down

Endless survival. Great place to farm random Gear drops.

🌟 Awakening & Rebirth

Level a 5⭐/6⭐ to 100 with 5 Ascensions to Awaken them. Beat Stage 20 to Rebirth for permanent multipliers!
