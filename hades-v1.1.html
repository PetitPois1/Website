The Underworld

Spike Traps

Floor plates that shoot up metal spikes on a timer. They pulse red before firing — dash through or go around. 12 damage if caught.

Lava Pits

Impassable molten rock. Walk around them — or if the gap is narrow enough, you can dash across.

Health Orbs

Smash the terracotta vases — 1-in-5 chance a red orb drops. Walk near it and it flies to you, restoring 20 HP.

Blade Combo / Bow Charge

Stygian Blade: Three pips under HP — hit twice then finish with a 2.5× power strike. Heart-Seeking Bow: Tap for quick arrows, hold to charge a piercing shot.
